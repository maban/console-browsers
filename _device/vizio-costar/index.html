Second generation Google TV. Runs Android and apps can be downloaded from Google Play.

The trackpad on the remote takes a lot of getting used to. Scrolling may seem the wrong way round to some users, and tracking is fast. Therefore users may find it easier to navigate using d-pad. Strongly recommend testing sites with the remote.

There are buttons on both sides of the remote. The back of the remote has a full QWERTY keyboard.
